The Wonder of the Web Series

There are people that are dedicated to animating entire shows on youtube,
some have even been picked up by larger studios due to the popularity
they've gained on youtube.

Bigtop Burger

Created by Worthikids

The premise revolves around the titular Bigtop Burger, a food truck that sells burgers with a circus clown motif, and the shenanigans that the truck's group gets into on their job, mostly because of their bizarre boss and their rival truck, Zomburger.

ENA

Created by Joel G

ENA is a surrealistic animation and game series created by Joel Guerra posted on YouTube and Newgrounds. It utilizes a mix of 2D and 3D animation that takes the viewer on a surreal journey through bizarre worlds and environments, meeting equally strange characters and situations through the perspective of the series's titular characters.

Bee and PuppyCat

Created by Natasha Allegri

The series revolves around Bee, a reluctant hero, becomes entangled in the adventures of a mysterious creature named PuppyCat. Together they travel between reality and the void of Fishbowl Space. Created by Natasha Allegri, character designer and storyboard artist for Adventure Time. The show was originally posted on youtube on the Cartoon Hangover channel in 2013, but has recently been adapted into an animated Netflix series in 2022.

Eddsworld

Created by Edd Gould

Eddsworld is a British 2D Flash-animated web series created by Edd Gould. Since its premiere on 6 June 2003, the series has been published through Newgrounds, DeviantArt, YouTube, Albino Blacksheep, Tumblr, SheezyArt, and an official website. Eddsworld follows the misadventures of a group of young adult "morons' ' living together in a house somewhere in London.
